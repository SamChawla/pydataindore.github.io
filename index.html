About PyData Indore 2025

Central India's premier conference for data science professionals and enthusiasts

Join the Data Revolution in Indore

PyData Indore 2025 brings together leading data scientists, engineers, analysts, and business leaders from across India and beyond to share knowledge, forge connections, and explore the latest trends in data science and AI—all in the vibrant commercial capital of Madhya Pradesh.

This three-day conference features inspiring keynote presentations, hands-on technical workshops, panel discussions, and exceptional networking opportunities. Together, we help you stay at the forefront of the rapidly evolving data landscape, right here in Indore—renowned for its education, tech innovation, and dynamic spirit.

Whether you're a seasoned data professional or just beginning your journey, PyData Indore 2025 offers valuable insights and partnerships to accelerate your career and drive real innovation in your organization.
